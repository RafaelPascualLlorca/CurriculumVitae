Automatic traffic light

In 4º de of ESO as an evaluative work, he had to make an automatic traffic light in a circuit of motherboard. With a manual, we had to put the components, weld them, and finally prove that the circuit is correct. To prove that we had to use a battery and some cables that we put inside of the circuit and taste it, the order was this: Green, yellow and red and inverse.
